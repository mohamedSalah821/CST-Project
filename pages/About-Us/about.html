About This Website

Welcome to our Multi-Actor E-commerce System! This platform is designed to provide a seamless online shopping experience for Customers, Sellers, and Admins.

Customers can browse products, manage their shopping carts, and place orders easily. Sellers can manage their products, track orders, and view sales analytics. Admins have full control to manage users and moderate listings.

Customer Experience

Easy product browsing, secure checkout, and order tracking for customers.

Seller Tools

Manage products, view analytics, and process orders efficiently.

Admin Control

Full management of users, product moderation, and platform oversight.

Our Mission

To create a secure, user-friendly, and fully functional e-commerce system for all actors.

Our Vision

To become a reliable platform for online shopping and multi-actor interaction.
